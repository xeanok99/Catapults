Types of catapults

There are four types of catapults-:
1.Ballista
2.Onager
3.Trebuchet

Ballista

This was a large crossbow-like device that used torsion springs (twisted ropes) to launch bolts or spears.

Onager

It was a type of catapult that used a torsion-powered arm to hurl stones or other projectiles.

Trebuchet

A counterweight-powered catapult that used a swinging arm to launch projectiles. Trebuchets were particularly effective and could launch heavy projectiles with great force.
